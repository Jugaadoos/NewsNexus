# User Manual / Help Center / FAQ

## 1) Getting started
1. Open the app.
2. Sign up or log in.
3. Set preferences and location context.
4. Browse personalized feed and open summaries.

## 2) Common actions
- Save or revisit stories through your profile/workflow pages.
- Upgrade subscription from available subscription options.
- Use role-specific pages (analytics, partner portal, affiliate dashboard) based on permissions.

## 3) FAQ

### Q: Why does article detail vary by source?
Some source feeds provide limited metadata. The app enriches where possible, but source quality can vary.

### Q: What happens if AI summary fails?
The app should still show core article information with fallback behavior.

### Q: Why am I seeing ads?
Ad display may depend on your subscription tier and app settings.

### Q: Can I update preferences?
Yes. Use profile/settings areas to update personalization details.

### Q: How can I report issues?
Use support/contact channels defined by your deployment operator.

## 4) Advanced role notes
- Reviewers/editors: use moderation flows and record clear decision rationale.
- Partners/affiliates: monitor analytics and conversion trends for optimization.
- Admins: regularly verify role access and system health.
