# Terms of Purchase (Draft)

_Last updated: 2026-02-11_

> Important: Draft template for product context; requires legal review.

## 1. Scope
Applies to paid subscriptions and any paid offerings in NewsNexus.

## 2. Pricing and billing
- Prices are shown before purchase confirmation.
- Billing cadence and renewal terms must be shown at checkout.

## 3. Cancellations
Users may cancel recurring plans per the subscription settings flow. Access behavior after cancellation should be shown clearly.

## 4. Refunds
Refund decisions depend on policy, jurisdiction, and provider terms. Current product behavior must be documented in-app.

## 5. Payment processors
Payments may be processed by third-party providers (e.g., Stripe), and their terms also apply.

## 6. Disputes
Chargeback/dispute handling follows provider procedures and applicable law.
