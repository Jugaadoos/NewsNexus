# Data Processing Agreement (DPA) (Draft)

_Last updated: 2026-02-11_

> Important: Draft operational template; must be reviewed by legal counsel.

## 1. Parties and roles
- Controller: Customer/organization determining data purposes.
- Processor: NewsNexus operating service infrastructure.

## 2. Subject matter
Processing of personal data necessary to provide platform services.

## 3. Categories of data
- Account identifiers.
- Usage and analytics events.
- Content interaction and preference data.

## 4. Processor obligations
- Process only on documented instructions.
- Apply reasonable security measures.
- Ensure confidentiality and access controls.
- Assist with data subject rights where required.

## 5. Subprocessors
Subprocessor usage must be disclosed and managed through appropriate agreements.

## 6. Incident handling
Processor should notify controller of relevant data incidents without undue delay.

## 7. Deletion/return
Upon termination, data should be returned or deleted according to agreement and law.
