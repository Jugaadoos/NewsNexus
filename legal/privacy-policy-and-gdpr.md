# Privacy Policy & GDPR Compliance Notes (Draft)

_Last updated: 2026-02-11_

> Important: Draft for engineering/product alignment; legal counsel should finalize jurisdiction-specific language.

## 1. Data collected
- Account data (email, role, settings).
- Usage and analytics data (events and interactions).
- Content interaction metadata (reads, clicks, preferences).
- Optional location context where enabled.

## 2. Purpose of processing
- Provide product functionality.
- Personalize content and improve relevance.
- Support billing, security, and fraud prevention.
- Generate aggregate analytics.

## 3. Legal basis (GDPR context)
- Contractual necessity for core account/service operations.
- Legitimate interest for product improvement and security.
- Consent where required by law (especially for optional tracking).

## 4. User rights
Users may request access, correction, deletion, portability, and objection per applicable law.

## 5. Data retention
Retention periods should be documented and enforced for each data category.

## 6. Data sharing
Data may be shared with processors (e.g., infrastructure, AI, payments) only as needed to deliver services.

## 7. International transfers
If data moves across regions, appropriate safeguards should be documented.

## 8. Security
Reasonable technical and organizational measures are expected; see infrastructure/security documentation.

## 9. Contact and updates
Policy updates should be versioned and announced in release notes when materially changed.
